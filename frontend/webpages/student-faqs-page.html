Student FAQs

Joining Courses

How do I join a course?

Click the "Join Course" button on your homepage. You can join public courses directly or enter a code for private courses.

Is there a code for private courses?

Yes, enter the code if it exists.

Activities

Can I retry an activity if I fail?

Yes, you can retry until you get the correct solution.

Leaderboard

How are rankings decided?

Rankings are based on total points earned and fastest completion times.

Progress

Can I track my progress?

Yes, your completed activities and achievements are shown in your profile.
