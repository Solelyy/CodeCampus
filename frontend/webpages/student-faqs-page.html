Student FAQs

How do I enroll in a course?

Go to the Courses tab and click “Enroll” next to the course you want to join.

Where can I see my grades?

Grades are available in the Dashboard under the “My Grades” section.

Can I message my professor?

Yes! Use the Messaging tab to send direct messages to your instructors.

How do I reset my password?

Go to Account Settings and click “Change Password.” Follow the instructions provided.

What if I miss a deadline?

Contact your professor immediately. Some courses allow extensions depending on the situation.
