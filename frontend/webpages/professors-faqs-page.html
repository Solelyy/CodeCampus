Professor FAQs

How do I create a new course?

From your dashboard, click "Create Course" and fill in the details.

How do I add activities with correct answers?

Use the "Activities" tab inside a course to set the problem statement and test cases.

How can I track students in a course?

Go to the "Students" tab in a private course to see who joined and their progress.

Can I monitor student performance?

Yes, detailed analytics are available under the course dashboard.
